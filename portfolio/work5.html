Typography Composition

Winter 2017

Challenge: Find 4 unique characteristics of the typeface you chose. Create a composition that highlights those parts

Solution: For this project, the typeface I chose was Bodoni. The reason why I chose Bodoni is because of the great contrast of its thin and thick strokes. Another reason why I chose Bodoni because it represents who I am as a designer. I love to be bold and stand out as a designer. Throughout the process, I was obsessed with perfection. I wanted the lines of the letters to create a line or connection to the next letters. As I continue to create multiple iterations, I got out of my comfort zone and tried a disconnection between the letters. Even though not all lines are connected, the letters in the composition are dynamic and strong. I wasn't afraid to zoom in to the very core of the letter, but I was still able to make them recognizeable. In the end, my composition created an interesting shape.

Tools:
Adobe Illustrator
