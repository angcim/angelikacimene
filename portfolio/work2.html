Typography Poster

Fall 2017

Challenge: Choose a typeface and create a typographic poster that accurately represents that typeface.

Solution: For this project, I chose Garamond as my typeface. The reason why I chose Garamond is because it is such an elegant font. It has many unique shapes and characteristics such its serifs. Because Garamond is a tradition and old-style typeface, I chose colors that are old and classical, such as a brownish/orangey color. To add an ancient effect, I added a black shadows. In order to accurately represent this typeface in my poster, I wrote down all the ABCs with the whole font family to show the elegance and legibility that Garamond offers. Garamond is known for long text and passages, so I wanted to show its readability through my poster. I also added some of its history such as it being an old style serif and that it was made in 1530.

Tools:
Adobe Illustrator
