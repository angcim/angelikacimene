About Me

Welcome to my portfolio website. My name is Angelika Cimene. I am a 3rd year Design major and Professional Writing minor at University of California, Davis. My main interests includes Graphic Design, UI/UX, Web Design/Development, and Marketing. An ambition of mine is to become one of the best creative directors just like Don Draper in Mad Men.
